[top] Jain Roulston model from sdevice

FILE: Default.tcl
EXPRESSION: 

Bandgap narrowing  is calculated using
deltaEg = A_i N^(1/3) + B_i N^(1/4) + C_i N^(1/2) + D_i N^(1/2)
where i is n for n-type or p for p-type

CALCULATED PARAMETER: A_i, B_i, C_i, D_i, deltaEg [eV]
VALIDITY: This model is valid for both n-Ge and p-Ge
REFERENCE:
S.C.Jain and D.J.Roulston, "A Simple Expression for Band Gap Narrowing (BGN) in Heavily Doped Si, Ge, Ge and Ge(x)Si(1-x) Strained Layers," Solid-State Electronics, vol. 34, no.5, pp.453-465, 1991
