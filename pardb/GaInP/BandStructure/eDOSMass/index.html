[top] Temperature dependent model from Levinshtein

FILE: Levinshtein.tcl
EXPRESSION: 

Temperature dependent electron DOS is used to calculate the electron DOS mass using
mn/m0 = (Nc/2)^(2/3) * (h^2/2*pi*kB*T*mn*m0)

CALCULATED PARAMETER: mm returns mn/m0 [1]
VALIDITY:
REFERENCE:
"Handbook series on Semiconductor Parameters, Vol I" M. Levenshtein, S. Rumyantsev, M. Shur, World Scientific, 1996
NOTES: Formula 1 is used to calculate the electron DOS and DOS mass.
