[top] Temperature dependent model from Levinshtein

FILE: Default.tcl
EXPRESSION: 

The electron DOS effective mass is used to calcualate the electron DOS using
Nc = 2*(2*pi*kB*T*mn*m0/h^2)^(3/2)

CALCULATED PARAMETER: mm returns mn/m0 [1]
VALIDITY: This model is valid only for x=0.5
REFERENCE:
Gergoe's Material Parameter Database
NOTES: Electron DOS effective mass is used to calculate temperature dependent electron DOS. Formula 1 is used to calculate the electron DOS and DOS mass.
